Why Refinance?

Lower Interest Rate

Rates, fees and lender incentives change regularly. We compare your current loan against suitable alternatives and show whether the potential benefit outweighs switching costs.

Shorten Your Loan Term

Refinancing can support a shorter loan term, but only if repayments remain comfortable. We show the trade-off between faster repayment and monthly cash flow.

Cash Out Equity

If your property has increased in value, you may have equity to access. Refinance to cash out for renovations, debt consolidation, or a deposit on your next investment.

Cashback Offers

Some lenders offer refinance incentives. We factor conditions, fees and long-term value into the comparison rather than relying on the upfront offer alone.

Better Loan Features

Need an offset account, unlimited redraw, or split loan options? We'll find a lender that offers the features you need — not just the lowest rate.
