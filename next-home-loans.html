Your Next Home Journey

Upsizing

Growing your family or needing more space? We assess equity, borrowing capacity and settlement timing so you can compare selling first, buying first or using bridging finance.

Downsizing

Downsizing may release equity, reduce debt or change your repayment needs. We help you test the lending options before you commit to a purchase timeline.

Relocation & Sea Change

Moving interstate or seeking a lifestyle change? We work with clients across Australia and can help you understand different state stamp duty rates, land tax implications, and lender policies in your new location.

Porting Your Loan

Loan portability may be available, but it is not automatic. We review your current product, lender rules and market alternatives before recommending a path.

Capital Gains & Stamp Duty

Selling your investment or family home triggers capital gains tax and stamp duty on your purchase. We'll coordinate with your accountant to calculate the true cost and structure your finance accordingly.
