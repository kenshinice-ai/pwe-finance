What We Help With

First Home Owner Grant

The First Home Owner Grant (FHOG) provides eligible first home buyers with a grant of up to $10,000 depending on your state. In Victoria, you may receive $10,000 towards the purchase of a new or substantially renovated home valued under $600,000.

Stamp Duty Concessions

First home buyers in Victoria may be eligible for stamp duty concessions or exemptions on residential property purchases up to $600,000. Full exemption applies for properties up to $300,000. We'll calculate exactly what you save.

Low Deposit Loans (5% or Less)

You don't need a 20% deposit to buy your first home. Many lenders offer loans with as little as 5% deposit, and the First Home Guarantee scheme allows eligible buyers to purchase with just a 5% deposit without paying LMI.

Lender's Mortgage Insurance (LMI)

If you're depositing less than 20%, LMI protects the lender — not you. We'll find lenders offering competitive LMI rates and may access government guarantee schemes to help you avoid it entirely.
