What We Help With

First Home Owner Grant

First home buyer grants may be available depending on the property, location and eligibility rules. We help you understand the common pathways and confirm what needs to be checked before applying.

Stamp Duty Concessions

Victorian stamp duty concessions and exemptions can change and depend on property value, buyer status and eligibility. We help you factor these costs into your deposit and approval planning.

Low Deposit Loans (5% or Less)

Some buyers may qualify for lower-deposit options, but lender policy, LMI, government guarantee rules and repayment comfort all matter. We compare what is realistic for your situation.

Lender's Mortgage Insurance (LMI)

If you're depositing less than 20%, LMI protects the lender — not you. We compare lender policy, LMI treatment and any relevant guarantee pathways so you can see the true upfront and monthly cost.

First Home Buyer Roadmap

We create a personalised roadmap covering savings strategy, borrowing capacity assessment, property search guidance, pre-approval process, and settlement — all tailored to your unique financial situation.
