Home/About Us

Our Story

PWE Finance was founded with a simple mission: to provide honest, expert mortgage broking services that put clients first. Based in Melbourne, we've grown from a small local practice into a team of dedicated finance professionals serving clients across Australia.

We work for you — not the big banks, lenders or other financial institutions. We always have, and always will. This means we compare thousands of loan products from over 90 lenders to find the best deal for your unique situation.

Whether you're buying your first home, expanding your investment portfolio, or securing commercial finance — our team brings deep market knowledge and a personal touch to every client relationship.

Our Values

Best Interests First

We research the market, negotiate on your behalf and always recommend what's best for you — not what earns us the highest commission.

Your Time Matters

We do the legwork — from application to settlement. Meet on your terms, online or in person.

Your Partner Always

We're here for your today, tomorrow and future goals. On hand when you need it — we don't close at 4pm.
